Private Dining & Events

Looking for the perfect space to host your next special occasion At AS-Foods, we offer elegant private dining experiences tailored to your needs. Whether you’re planning an intimate dinner, a corporate event, or a festive celebration, our private dining rooms provide the ideal setting for any occasion.

Mezzanine

Situated on the upper floor, our mezzanine can accommodate up to 50 guests seated and 60 guests standing. This spacious and light-filled space commands impressive vistas over the main dining room, whilst views of the garden can be glimpsed through the room’s impressive arched windows. The floating lights above the bar counter lend further drama to this area.

Banking Hall

The opulence of the existing architecture of the Banking Hall is complemented by rich upholstery tones, elaborate details, impactful lighting and elegant, comfortable seating. Private hire for up to 180 guests seated available upon individual request.

Book a room

Looking for the perfect venue for your next business meeting, presentation, or private event? Consider the exclusive private areas at Piazza Italiana. Variety of our spaces are ideal for dry hire, offering a sophisticated and professional setting tailored to your needs.
